Property Lease-Up Summary Dashboard

This dashboard presents an interactive overview of property statuses across two distinct markets. It showcases two maps, each marking all properties, with delivered properties distinguished by a different color. Users can examine rent growth trends over 3, 6, 12, and 24 months for each market, accessible through intuitive buttons. The secondary page provides detailed statistics and charts, specifically focusing on the delivered properties.
